Employment Equity Committee Training

Employment Equity (EE) fails when it's treated as paperwork. We cut the noise, align your committee on what's required, how to consult properly, and how to build an audit-ready EE Plan that stands up to scrutiny and supports your talent strategy.

Quick Facts Strip

  • Delivery: In-person • Virtual • Hybrid
  • Scheduling: On request (no fixed public dates)
  • Durations: Half-day (4h) or Full-day (7h)
  • Audience: EE Committee members, HR, line managers, EXCO sponsors, compliance
  • Pricing (ex VAT): In-house ≤20 — R10,000 half-day | R18,000 full-day. Virtual private ≤20 — R8,500 half-day | R15,500 full-day.

Outcome: A committee that knows its role, Consults correctly and keeps an evidence file that passes inspection.

Curriculum / What You Will Learn

  • Committee purpose & roles: Chair, HR, Line, Organised Labour (who does what, when).
  • Legal essentials: What the Act requires vs What inspectors check in practice.
  • Consultation done right: Agendas, Notices, Minutes, Resolving Objections.
  • EE Plan & numerical goals: How to set goals.
  • Align to sector targets: Show "reasonable progress."
  • Workforce & barrier analysis: Collecting evidence, Identifying Barriers, Crafting Realistic measures.
  • Evidence & record-keeping: What belongs in the file (templates included).
  • Justifications for gaps: Documenting reasoned explanations to support certification decisions.
  • Governance cadence: Quarterly meetings, Owners, Dashboards, Pre-verification checks.
  • Do / Don't: No quotas, No window-dressing; Focus on Lawful, Defensible actions.

Deliverables (You Keep Them)

  • EE Committee Charter & Agenda templates
  • Minutes template with action tracking
  • EE Plan outline with goals & measures
  • Barrier Analysis Matrix
  • Evidence File Checklist (audit-ready)
  • Compliance Calendar (meetings, submissions, checks)

Optional Add-Ons

  • Sector targets briefing tailored to your industry
  • Policy & recruitment audit mini-checklist (bias & barrier scan)
  • EE reporting clinic ahead of submission (virtual Q&A, 60 min)
