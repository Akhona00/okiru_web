AI Compliance Agents Explained

What Are AI Agents?

AI Agents are smarter than traditional bots. They combine artificial intelligence and automation to understand context, adapt to data and complete multi-step business processes.

They're More Powerful Than Bots

  • Context-aware: Go beyond scripts and adapt to business needs.
  • Multi-step workflows: Automate full processes, not just single tasks.
  • Continuous learning: Improve accuracy and relevance over time.
  • Decision support: Deliver insights for smarter, faster choices.
