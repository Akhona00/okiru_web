Align Compliance, Strategy and Impact with Okiru

At Okiru Consulting, we help organisations integrate Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) principles into their business strategy. From net-zero planning and ESG reporting to risk management, due diligence, and stakeholder engagement, we provide practical, compliant and future-focused solutions. Our approach ensures that ESG is not just about compliance, but a driver of long-term value, resilience and competitive advantage.

What Okiru’s Sustainability and ESG Consulting Can Do for You

In South Africa, sustainability and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) considerations are no longer optional. They are being driven by government regulations, investor expectations, customer sentiment, B-BBEE compliance and the views of employees and communities. At Okiru Consulting, we help businesses embed ESG into their core strategy, turning compliance into a driver of growth, resilience and transformation. Whether you are a corporate, SME, financial institution or government body, our team will help you design and deliver a sustainability and ESG strategy that creates measurable impact and protects long-term value

Your ESG Challenges, Our Support

  • Decarbonisation & Net-Zero Planning: Transitioning to sustainable energy models and reducing emissions.
  • Sustainable Procurement & Supply Chains: Aligning sourcing with ESG and B-BBEE requirements, ensuring fair labour and human rights.
  • Inclusive Growth: Meeting Employment Equity and skills development targets while advancing workplace diversity.
  • Sustainable Finance: Accessing green finance, restructuring portfolios and aligning capital allocation with ESG goals.
  • Climate & Social Risk: Assessing exposure to environmental, social and governance risks and building resilience.

How Okiru Helps You Deliver ESG Ambitions

  • ESG Strategy & Transformation: Reframe your strategy to integrate sustainability into decision-making and operations.
  • ESG Reporting & Disclosure: Ensure compliance with local legislation, SETA/QCTO standards, B-BBEE scorecard, and global reporting frameworks.
  • Skills & Social Impact: Build ESG-linked skills pipelines and measurable socio-economic development initiatives.
  • Capital & Finance Advisory: Support on green infrastructure, sustainable investments, and tax implications of ESG.
