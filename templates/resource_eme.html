Exempt Micro Enterprise BBBEE Affidavits

If your business has an annual turnover of less than R10 million, it qualifies as an Exempt Micro Enterprise (EME) under the BBBEE Codes of Good Practice. EMEs automatically receive BBBEE Level 4 status without the need for a full verification audit.

Ownership can boost your BBBEE level even further:

  • 51% or more black ownership = BBBEE Level 2 recognition.
  • 100% black ownership = BBBEE Level 1 recognition.

To confirm your status, you’ll need a sworn BBBEE EME Affidavit – a simple, official document you can download and complete in minutes Whether you’re applying for tenders, securing contracts or updating your compliance records, your BBBEE EME affidavit is essential proof of your BEE level
