Why the Okiru B-BBEE Scorecard Training

B-BBEE is only a burden when it's misunderstood. We teach teams how the scorecard really works, then give you the tools to run it. Sessions are on request and delivered in-person, virtual, or hybrid. Let us cut the noise, explain the rules in plain English and show your team exactly how to stay audit-ready while driving real business outcomes.

Quick Facts Strip

  • Delivery: In-person • Virtual • Hybrid
  • Scheduling: On request
  • Durations: Half-day (4h) or Full-day (7h)
  • Audience: HR/EE, Procurement, Finance, Compliance, Line Managers, Executives
  • Pricing (ex VAT): In-house ≤20 — R10,000 half-day | R18,000 full-day. Virtual private ≤20 — R8,500 half-day | R15,500 full-day

Intermediate B-BBEE Training (Workshops)

What you'll learn :

  • Generic vs Sector Codes, Elements, Weightings, Priority Rules
  • What counts for points across Procurement, Management Control, Skills Development, ESD/SED, YES4Youth and Ownership.
  • Evidence that sticks: Affidavits/Certificates, Sampling Logic, File Structure, Naming Controls
  • Setting realistic targets and tracking reasonable progress

Who it's for: HR/EE Coordinators, Procurement & Finance Teams, Compliance Officers, Line Managers

What's included: Slides, Master Evidence Checklist, Verification File Starter Kit (folders + naming), 30/60/90-day action outline

Note: This track does not include execution of B-BBEE initiatives.

B-BBEE Manager Programme (Advanced)

What you'll master :

  • Element-by-element decision frameworks with scenario testing
  • Budgeting for Skills/ESD/SED and procurement pipeline design
  • Building and maintaining an audit-proof verification file
  • Governance cadence: roles, approvals, dashboards, pre-verification checks
  • Workbooks & Exercises (you keep them):
  • Scorecard Calculator & Target-Setting workbook
  • Master Evidence Checklist + templated folder structure
  • Verification File Build-along (guided case; "verifier's view")
  • Action Plan playbook (owners, timelines, proof required)

Delivery & Pricing: Cohort (in-house/virtual). Quoted per cohort based on group size, coaching hours and deliverables.

Typical cadence: 2 - 3 full-day sessions over 3 - 4 weeks + post-programme Q&A clinic.

Note: This track does include exercises and tools to execute your B-BBEE strategy and plan.
