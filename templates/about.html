By combining deep sector expertise with AI-powered compliance agents, we turn complex requirements into clear, actionable strategies that unlock measurable business value Compliance in South Africa is complex, high-stakes and constantly shifting. At Okiru, we believe it shouldn’t slow your business down. That’s why we built a compliance ecosystem where AI and human expertise work side by side. Our AI Compliance Agents automate evidence gathering, scorecard tracking and reporting, while our consultants guide strategy and implementation. The result: faster submissions, fewer errors and more B-BBEE points on the board.

Okiru Methodology

Understand Your Baseline

We analyse your current B-BBEE scorecard, Employment Equity, and Skills Development data with AI-powered tools. This reveals compliance gaps, risks and opportunities so you know exactly where you stand.

Design Your Compliance Roadmap

We turn insights into a practical plan. By aligning transformation goals with business priorities, we structure reporting processes that cut turnaround times, reduce audit risk and maximise B-BBEE points.

Put the Plan Into Action

Our consultants and AI Compliance Agents streamline data capture, automate reporting and support submissions like WSP/ATR and bursary management. The result: faster execution and audit-ready outputs.

Monitor, Measure, Improve

We track results continuously, adapting to regulatory changes and finding new efficiencies. With ongoing optimisation, you sustain compliance, reduce costs and keep improving your transformation outcomes.
