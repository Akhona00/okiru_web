B-BBEE Verification Tracking

Use Case: Create a dedicated “Verification” pipeline in the CRM where each element of the B-BBEE scorecard (Management Control, Skills Development, ESD, SED) is a stage. How it Works: Every time supporting evidence, like payroll data or supplier affidavits is uploaded, an automated task notifies the responsible manager. A dashboard displays percentage completion and upcoming submission deadlines. Executives can log in at any time to see real-time verification status and outstanding items.

Employment Equity Reporting Oversight

Use Case: Automate Employment Equity (EEA2/EEA4) data collection. How it Works: Managers receive automated reminders to submit workforce demographic updates. The CRM consolidates these into a single report for HR and flags any gaps before the Department of Employment and Labour deadline.

Skills Development & WSP/ATR Management

Use Case: Map learnerships and bursary spend inside the CRM to monitor SETA grant claims. How it Works: Each learner is a “record” with fields for funding, start/end dates, and proof-of-enrolment documents. Automated scoring highlights whether the company is on track for Skills Development points and sends alerts if claims are approaching expiry.

Client & Supplier Vetting for ESD

Use Case: Integrate a supplier portal with the CRM to capture B-BBEE certificates and track expiry. How it Works: Suppliers upload certificates directly; the CRM verifies date validity, sets renewal reminders, and feeds a live procurement-compliance dashboard.

Client & Supplier Vetting for ESD

Use Case: Trigger campaigns based on compliance milestones. How it Works: When a verification stage hits 75 % completion, an automated email series updates executives and invites them to a strategy session, ensuring decision-makers stay engaged without manual follow-ups.
